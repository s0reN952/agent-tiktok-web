Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

AutoPoster ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data We Collect

The App only accesses OAuth tokens and API credentials that you explicitly provide. These are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

2. Third-Party Services

The App connects to third-party APIs as authorized by you. Your use of those services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

3. Data Storage

All configuration and credentials are stored locally in a configuration file on your own device. We do not maintain any remote database of user data.

4. Your Rights

You can delete all app data at any time by removing the application and its configuration files from your device.
